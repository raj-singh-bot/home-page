ASPHALT
When it comes to shingles there are three types to choose from. 3 Tab Shingles, Dimensional shingles, and Luxury Shingles. Our team is knowledgeable and experienced and can help you choose the right shingle for your business, institution, or personal home.
SLATE
We have years of experience in working with slate. We can source a wide variety of natural slate options in a wide array of colors to create the look you want. We are also highly skilled in slate restoration work.
SPECIALITY TILE
We want our customers to have exactly what they want. That’s why we offer a wide selection of speciality roof tiles in various styles, colors, and materials. We are only too happy to talk you through some of the options allowing you to make an informed choice that is right for you and your property.
CEDAR
We have extensive experience in installing all types of cedar roofing and generally have at least one crew installing cedar every work day of the year. Gillespie Contracting Inc is certified through The Cedar Shake & Shingle Bureau.
METAL
At Gillespie Contracting Inc we offer a variety of custom metal fabrication using a range of materials including copper, steel, zinc, and aluminum. In addition to metal roofing, this includes finials, window fabrication, roof crests, flashing and custom gutters.
HISTORICAL RESTORATION
At Gillespie Contracting Inc. we believe that restoration is an art form. We have 30+ years of experience which has led us to the forefront of the industry. We specialize in historic restoration work of homes, churches, university buildings and other historic structures.
SIDING
We offer a range of siding installation, siding repair and siding replacement services carried out by experienced siding contractors.
WINDOWS
In addition to roofing services, we can also offer a range of window installation services, including custom metal window fabrication.
GUTTERS
We use World Gutter System for all our Euro style gutter systems. We are also capable of fabricating custom gutters, k-style, and half round style using copper for a durable and attractive finish to any property.
